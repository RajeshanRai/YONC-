Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Our team works Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

Can I become a partner?

Absolutely! We're always looking for organizations and experts to join our network. Select "Partnership" in the subject field and tell us about your services.

Is my information secure?

Yes, all information submitted through this form is encrypted and kept confidential. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I request emergency support?

For emergency situations, please contact the appropriate local authorities or call 911 in Canada. Use "Technical Support" in the form for urgent platform issues.
