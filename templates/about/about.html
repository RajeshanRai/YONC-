About Youth of Nepal in Canada

We connect Nepali youth in Canada with trusted experts, community support, and meaningful resources.

Youth of Nepal in Canada is a community-first platform built to help Nepali students, professionals, and families thrive across provinces. We bring together verified experts, trusted service partners, and local community guidance in one safe space.

Who We Are

We are a Canadian Nepali youth network dedicated to helping newcomers and long-term residents navigate life in Canada. Our members include students, professionals, and community advocates who need expert advice on everything from immigration to mental health.

Our platform makes it easy to find qualified experts, trusted services, and emergency resources in every province. We help people connect, learn, and feel supported throughout their Canadian journey.

How We Started

Youth of Nepal in Canada began as a small community initiative to support Nepali students and families when they first arrive. We saw a need for better guidance, trusted experts, and accessible support across provinces, so we built a platform to bring it all together.

Over time, we grew into a larger network of experts, community partners, and users who share the same mission: help Nepali youth in Canada feel confident, informed, and connected.

Our Partners

We work with expert advisors, student support groups, community organizations, and service providers across Canada to create a trusted support network for Nepali youth.
